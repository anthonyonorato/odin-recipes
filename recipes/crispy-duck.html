Crispy Duck

Blackberry Bourbon Sauce

Few things are as wonderful as a perfectly cooked piece of duck with crispy skin

This is a great dish to get comfortable with making your own sauces, as well as tackling a dish that many people seem intimidated by...duck!

This dish will take over 2 hours to prepare, but actual working time is closer to 35 minutes.

Here's what you need:

  • 2 7oz Duck Breasts
  • 3-4 Large sweet potatos
  • 1 Package of blackberries
  • 2 Tbsp of cherry preserves
  • 2 oz of bourbon
  • 1 Shallot
  • 4 Tbsp of butter
  1. Score the skin of the duck in either lines or a cross-hatched pattern(this allows the fat to render)
  2. Cut the tomatoes in half, cut the the mozzarella. Mix them with the pasta. Add fresh basil, serve fresh!
